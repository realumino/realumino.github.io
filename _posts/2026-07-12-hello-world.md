Welcome to my blog! This is the first post.

This blog is built with [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/) and hosted on [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/). I chose this setup because it's simple, free, and gives me full control over my content.

## Why start a blog?

I've been meaning to start a blog for a while. There's something special about having your own space on the internet — a place to think out loud, share what you're learning, and connect with others.

Some things I plan to write about:

- **Technology** — things I'm building and learning
- **Design** — thoughts on minimalism and user experience
- **Life** — reflections and random musings
